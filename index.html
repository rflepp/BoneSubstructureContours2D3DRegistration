Automatic Multi-view X‑ray/CT Registration

A novel, fully automated Registrierungsmethode für intraoperative Anwendung mit sub‑Millimeter‑Genauigkeit.

Paper Code

Abstract

Accurate intraoperative X‑ray/CT registration is essential for surgical navigation in orthopedic procedures. However, existing methods struggle with robustness or require manual steps. We propose a contour-based ICP optimization focusing on bone substructures to reduce ambiguity and operate fully automatically using only two X‑ray images, achieving sub‑millimeter accuracy and user-friendly integration into surgical workflows.
